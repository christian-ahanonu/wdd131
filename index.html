Abuja, Nigeria

Official Flag of Nigeria

Abuja is the capital and eighth most populous city of Nigeria. Situated in the middle of the country within the Federal Capital Territory (FCT), it is a planned city built mainly in the 1980s based on a master plan by International Planning Associates (IPA). Abuja Geography is defined by Aso Rock (a 400-metre monolith). In addition, there are other interesting areas such as Zuma Rock (a 792-metre monolith), Presidential Complex, National Assembly, Supreme Court, etc.
